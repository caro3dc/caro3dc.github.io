Viewership


What is your level of viewership?

Based on the responses we have for the second stream, some of our viewers lost focus or interest in the podcast due to ongoing schoolwork. Others said one of the hosts was stunning, others were trying to identify someone, and the rest didn't lose interest in the podcast.

Comparing these to the responses from our first stream, some also said that schoolworks were also the cause for them losing interest or focus in the podcast, some said it was because of the sloppy screen that caused distortion, and most of them said they didn't lose any focus or interests in the podcast.

Therefore, we can say that there were no impactful differences that determined our level of viewership this time around.
